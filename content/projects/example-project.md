## Overview

This project explores the intersection of branding and digital experience design. The goal was to create a cohesive visual identity that communicates trust, creativity, and innovation.

## Process

The design process began with extensive research into the target audience and competitive landscape. Key steps included:

- **Discovery**: Stakeholder interviews and market research
- **Ideation**: Mood boards, sketching, and concept development
- **Design**: High-fidelity mockups and prototyping
- **Refinement**: User testing and iterative improvements

## Outcome

The final deliverable included a complete brand identity system with logo variations, color palette, typography guidelines, and application examples across digital and print media.
